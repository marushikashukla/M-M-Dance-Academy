Styles We teach

We offer training in Semiclassical, Freestyle, and Bollywood Dance for All Ages and Every Occasion.

Semi-Classical

Embark on a graceful journey that blends tradition with artistic expression. Our semi-classical dance classes offer a harmonious fusion of classical Indian dance elements with a contemporary touch, celebrating the rich cultural heritage.

Freestyle

Set your spirit free and express yourself through the vibrant beats of freestyle dance. Unleash creativity, movement, and individuality in our dynamic classes that explore various styles, empowering you to dance with unbridled passion.

Bollywood

Step into the world of Bollywood magic! Our Bollywood dance sessions bring the energy and excitement of Indian cinema to life. From iconic moves to infectious rhythms, join us to experience the joy and glamour of Bollywood dance forms.
